### Best AI model in image analysis

On the side, I have also been doing some image analysis work using AI models provided through OpenRouter. I tested around 120 models using mainly medical item images, with the goal of extracting item names, barcodes, and manufacturer names from the images. Out of all the models I tested, Google Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite Preview delivered the best overall performance.

One interesting thing I found was that more expensive models do not necessarily perform the best overall. They often generate longer and more detailed responses than necessary for the task. Another interesting observation was that Qwen models (AI models developed in China) are usually three to five times cheaper than similarly sized AI models developed in the U.S. This may already be a well-known fact within the AI industry, but only after conducting this analysis did I realise how significant the price gap is and how much it could influence people’s choices when selecting models for their needs.
